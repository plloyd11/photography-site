Nice to meet you

Let's get to know one another.

Photography

The art of capturing a moment in time has always fascinated me. Coming from a family of photographers, I've always been drawn to images of far away landscapes, serene beauty, and peaceful somber.

Over the past few years I have become more and more obssessed with documenting the world around me - immersing myself in unique areas and exploring everything this planet has to offer.

Web Development & Design

For the past four years I have been honing my craft as a front-end web developer, with a focus on delivering clear, tangible user interfaces and delivering the best experience possible for my clients.

Nothing is better than being able to solve complex problems and display them in a clean, concise and creative way.

Music

It all began with the alto saxophone in elementary school and ever since I have had a passion for music and performance.

Switching to guitar in high school, I truly found the instrument for me. I've been obssessed with making guitar-driven music for the past 15 years, playing in numerous metal and punk bands in the tri-state area.

Recording

In 2009 I started recording friends bands in the New Brunswick basement scene. This lead me to create One Stone Recording & Mastering the same year. Ever since I have been helping bands throughout the tri-state area bring their artistic vision to life.
